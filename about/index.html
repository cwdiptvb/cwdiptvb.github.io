About Us

Free Live TV is a community-driven platform offering access to publicly available live streams across a variety of categories. Our goal is to make streaming simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone.

Whether you're into news, sports, movies, or music, we aim to provide a clean and user-friendly experience with no unnecessary clutter.

This project is built with open technologies and a passion for media accessibility. We welcome feedback and contributions from users who share our vision.
